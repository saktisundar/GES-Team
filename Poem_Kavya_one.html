My Nonsensical Dream!

Name : Kavya D

Class : VI

Day : Sunday

Date : 21.3.2021

I had a really good dream,
That was better than chocolate cream.
If you sit up and listen,
I’ll tell you what it means.

There were unicorns and termites everywhere,
And little kids with stuffed teddy bears.
Clowns were on roller skates,
Holding pictures of Bill Gates.
Books were flying,
Villains were crying,
In my nonsensical world.

But that’s not all,
There was also a ball.
In a castle so tall,
Even if you fall,
You would land on a feathery shawl.

Suddenly I heard a voice,
With such grace and poise.
When peeked through my eye lids,
I saw it was my mother Mrs Greenwids!
