Family

Name : Raeed

Class : VII B

Day : Tuesday

Date : 03.08.2021

I just see myself shining in the charm of the sun. I feel possessive about me that nobody must take me away except my family. This is because once when my grandpa was going down the escalator in a mall, I followed him, without letting the rest of the family knowing. I was a bit curious about him going alone then I lost track of him, I was so scared then I went near a bin closing my eyes suddenly I hear a sound saying “Raeed where are you” I opened my eyes and I saw my whole family near me they found me and cried that’s why I feel possessive about myself in the mirror. It doesn't need any change because I don’t want to leave my family who saved me in a crowded area they shine and charm me to the sun and that’s why anybody who has a caring family must feel possessive about yourself except you FAMILY!!!!!
