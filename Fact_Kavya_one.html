Amazing Facts About India You Never Knew!

Name : Kavya D

Class : VII B

Day : Tuesday

Date : 27.4.2021

India is the world’s second most populated country. It is also the seventh largest country. But did you know that India has the greatest number of post offices in the world? Or that it produces 70% of the world’s spices? Get ready for amazing facts about India!!

  1. India is home to two of the world’s oldest languages, Sanskrit and Tamil.
  2. India has never invaded or declared war first on other countries for 10000 years!
  3. Cows are considered holy in India. You heard me right!
    Cows are even allowed to roam freely on the streets!
  4. I am not finished with cows yet. In West Bengal (a state in India) all cows have identification cards!
  5. Do you like chess? Then you’ve got India to thank for! Chess, originally called “Chaturanga” originated in India.
  6. India has more vegetarians than the rest of the world! This is mainly having to do with religious purposes.
  7. Shampoo originated in India. But not in a luscious liquid state, it originated as a type of paste made of herbs. It was originally called “champu”.
  8. Kumbha Mela is a festival celebrated in India. It is the world’s largest gathering. It can also be seen from space!
  9. India has the lowest divorce rate.
  10. You might have heard of the legend of Atlantis- the mythical land which was submerged in the Atlantic Ocean. But did you know that India has its own Atlantis, except its real! Kumari Kandam was a landmass attached to southern India and was ruled by the Pandya’s until it was submerged under the Indian Ocean about five hundred years ago.
