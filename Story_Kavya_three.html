WHAT WOULD YOUR PLANT SAY?

Name : Kavya D

Class : VII B

Day : Tuesday

Date : 6.7.2021

Let me introduce myself. I’m plant 19 living in the vegetable patch, home garden. But to my owner I’m simply called “the brinjal plant”. I was born 6 months ago along with the other veggies.
“You’re lucky “said the old palm tree.
“Master hates brinjals. You won’t lose your vegetables”
The other plants also greeted me. Most of them told me it was rotten luck to be born during Covid 19.
“You won’t get any manure like we did” they say.
“What’s Covid 19” I ask.
“It’s a fever which animals like our master get” explained the sugarcane. “And it spreads”.
“How do you know this” asked my friend the chilli plant.
“I’m near the place where our master listens to a talking box called a TV”.
My life and master are perfect, but I always wonder why they’re deaf to me and my friends.
