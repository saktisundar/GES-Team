THE DREAMLAND

Name : Kavya D

Class : VII

Day : Monday

Date : 23.8.2021

Amelia woke up with a start. She had a bad nightmare. After looking around her room a few times she felt better. But just before Amelia closed her eyes, she spotted a glowing frisbee. Getting down from her bed, she grabbed it, not knowing what she had in wait.

Amelia blinked and she was no longer in her room. She looked around. To her amazement she was in a beach island, which had trampolines and water slides, surrounded by water with pink baby dolphins, a bubble machine, coconut trees, an outdoor barbecue and a sea jet!

What was more was, the coconut tree didn’t have coconuts, it had giant Choco bars! Amelia ran around this strange yet amazing place looking at its wonders. She paddled in the clear blue sea with the pink dolphins which were really friendly, rode on the sea jet, plucked the Choco-bars reaching them by bouncing on the trampoline, had a great time in water slides which led to the sea, ate barbecue and popped bubbles.

Then all of a sudden, the clouds started rumbling. Amelia knew it was going to rain so she looked around for shelter. As though the universe heard her wish, a large mansion appeared. She quickly went inside, finding a large galaxy themed playroom with a jacuzzi. But when she got in the jacuzzi, Amelia realized that there wasn’t any pitter-patter sound of the rain.

She ran out to find it was raining red and blue jellybeans outside. Amelia couldn’t believe her eyes and her tongue when she tasted one. After collecting some in her pocket she spent some more time on the beach and the waterslides when suddenly she woke up.

“Oh well, I should have known it was all a dream” said Amelia sadly as she looked around her room. But when she slipped her hands in her pockets while brushing her teeth, she found the red and blue jellybeans.
THE END
